Aashan ERP PWA files

Upload these files to GitHub:

1. app/layout.tsx
2. app/globals.css
3. public/manifest.json
4. public/icon-192.png
5. public/icon-512.png

For icons:
- Use your Aashan logo.
- Create two PNG files:
  - icon-192.png
  - icon-512.png
- Upload both inside the public folder.

After commit, Cloudflare will redeploy.
Then open ERP in iPhone Safari > Share > Add to Home Screen.
